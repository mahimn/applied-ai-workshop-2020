About the Bootcamp

This bootcamp is a deep dive into details of the deep learning architectures with a focus on learning end-to-end models for computer vision and NLP tasks. During the 6-day long course from 6th to 10th January 2020, you will learn to implement, train and debug your own neural networks and gain a detailed understanding of cutting-edge research in deep learning pertaining to computer vision and NLP.

Quick FAQs

What language will be used?

This bootcamp will utilize Python and its libraries. As a result, proficiency in Python is a necessity.

Who can register?

This bootcamp is open to all UTD students, with registration costing $25. This includes attendance to all six days of the bootcamp.

Any other prerequisites?

You are expected to have experience with college level calculus, linear algebra and statistics. This includes derivatives, matrix vector operations, probability basics, gaussian distributions, etc. You should also have some intermediate understanding of machine learning.

What do I need to bring?

All you need to bring to this workshop is any computing device with Python 3 (and preferably Anaconda) along with a willingness to learn!
